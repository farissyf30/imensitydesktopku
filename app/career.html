Join the imensity team!

 
Overview

Our team of engineers, designers, and marketing professionals is always growing. Since launching in Sweden in 2012, we’ve opened offices in Stockholm and New York City, and have built an intercontinental team that’s dedicated, hard-working, and always up for a challenge, adventure, or a laugh. We’re united by a passion for our vision, our product, and the technology that enables us to make imensity the best place to discover emerging brands from around the world. We’re also driven by the belief that it’s our community that defines us—and nothing excites us more than watching it grow.

Small businesses from over 140 countries around the world call imensity home. We know it’s the diversity of our community that makes it as strong and dynamic as it is, and it’s our ongoing mission to ensure that our workplaces reflect the same wide range of backgrounds, personalities, interests, skills, and stories. We want everyone in the imensity community—both online and in our offices—to feel that their needs are met, their voices are heard, and that they have all the necessary tools and opportunities to succeed.
