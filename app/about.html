Join our team





imensity was founded in 2018 by Edison in Australia. Led by CEO Carl W. Rivera, who'd watched his mother struggle to find an easy way to build an online shop for her ceramics, the group set out to develop a revolutionary tool to empower business owners everywhere to launch global brands.

Since then, a lot has changed. What's remained the same since the beginning. Though, is our mission to empower modern entrepreneurs to succeed. Interested in helping us shape the future of e-commerce?
