All about Imensity's referral program


To help you get started with our referral program, here are some more details on how it works.

For everyone
  • Customers cannot refer themselves for a discount code
  • Customers cannot combine these discounts with any other offers on Imensity
  • Discounts are valid for purchases made on Imensity.com only, with some restrictions**

** Referral discount codes are only valid for orders processed via Imensity Payments. Have a look at this article for more information.

For referrers

Do I need an account on Imensity to refer a friend?
No. Anyone can refer a friend to Imensity—thanks for spreading the word!

How do I get my 10% off?
As soon as the friend you refer makes their first purchase from Imensity’s marketplace, you’ll receive an email from us with your 10%-off code.

Please note that referral discount codes are only eligible for use on a refe rred customer’s first-time purchase. Anyone with an existing Imensity account—or who has made a prior purchase on Imensity.com or from a Imensity custom shop—is not eligible to redeem a referral discount code.

For referred customers

Do I need an account on Imensity to refer a friend?
No. Anyone can refer a friend to Imensity—thanks for spreading the word!
